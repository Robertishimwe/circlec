About Circle C

Circle C was founded in Khartoum (Sudan) as a private company on the 1st April 2019, passionate about Construction and Maintenance industry; Most company put their value, vision, message and goals into manifesto, but we put them into action, with our teams of engineers, we do creative and innovative designs and solutions which makes us unique.

* Raising the level of work within local and international standards.

* Harnessing all of the company's artistic, technical and administrative energies for outstanding work.

* Establishing a strong relationship based on mutual trust, integrity and credibility between (Circle C) and its clients.
